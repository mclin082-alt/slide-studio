Step 4 - Design the right response

Roadmap the asymmetry, not the incumbent checklist.

The sequence should first prove output quality in a narrow wedge, then widen toward editing, export, collaboration, and team workflows.

Now One-shot quality

Win high-quality generation for startup pitches, AI project showcases, product walkthroughs, technical proposals, and data stories.

Next Agentic editing

Make chat, annotation, current-scene edits, undo, and version history feel like editing with a presentation-aware coding agent.

Then Delivery formats

Strengthen PDF, HTML, share links, PNG, and PowerPoint export without letting static formats define the product.

Later Team systems

Add brand kits, reusable artifact templates, collaboration, reviewer comments, billing, and enterprise workflows.

The wedge

Make coding-agent power feel like a simple presentation workflow.

That is the asymmetric advantage: not generic AI slides, but expressive, specific, web-native artifacts for complex ideas.
